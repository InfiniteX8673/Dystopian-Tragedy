About Dystopian Tragedy

The World of Dystopian Tragedy

A Broken Future

In the year 2157, civilization lies in ruins. After the Great Collapse of 2089, humanity struggles to survive in a world dominated by rogue AI, corporate warlords, and mutated ecosystems. You are a survivor navigating the treacherous streets of Neo-Seattle, a city that never truly sleeps but has forgotten how to dream.

The Last Hope

As a member of the underground resistance, you carry the weight of humanity's future on your shoulders. Your mission: infiltrate the OmniCorp headquarters, retrieve the AI core that controls the city's systems, and restore freedom to a population that has known nothing but oppression for decades.

Choose Your Path

Every decision matters in this world. Will you be a stealthy ghost, leaving no trace as you dismantle the system from within? Or will you be a force of nature, bringing justice to the oppressed through combat and rebellion? The fate of Neo-Seattle rests in your hands.

Gameplay Mechanics

Combat System

Fast-paced tactical combat combining firearms, melee weapons, and cybernetic abilities. Master the art of gun-fu or become a stealth assassin.

Cybernetic Augmentations

Enhance your body with cutting-edge technology. Choose from over 50 different augmentations that affect gameplay and story outcomes.

Hacking & Stealth

Bypass security systems, manipulate cameras, and turn enemy technology against them. Multiple approaches to every objective.

Dynamic Dialogue

Your choices in conversations matter. Build relationships, gather intelligence, or intimidate your way to victory.

Development Journey

2024

Project Genesis

Started as a passion project by a small team of indie developers frustrated with the state of cyberpunk games.

2025

Alpha Development

Built the core systems, combat mechanics, and the first playable area of Neo-Seattle.

2026

Beta Release

Current version vBeta 0.1 features 5 main story missions, side quests, and multiplayer functionality.
