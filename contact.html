❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🖥️ What platforms is Dystopian Tragedy available on?

Dystopian Tragedy is currently available for Windows and Linux. We're working on Mac and console versions for future releases.

👥 Is there multiplayer support?

Yes! vBeta 0.1 includes co-op campaign for up to 4 players and competitive multiplayer modes. More multiplayer features are in development.

🐛 How can I report bugs or issues?

Use the contact form above or join our Discord server to report bugs. Please include as much detail as possible, including your system specifications.

🚀 Will there be future updates?

Absolutely! We're actively developing new content, features, and improvements. Follow our social media or join the newsletter for the latest updates.
